## Overview

Use this tool to generate random **rumors**, **quest hooks**, and **adventuring jobs** for your game. Select a category and party level, then roll to produce a ready-to-use adventure lead with appropriate reward scaling.

- **Rumor:** Vague hearsay â€” unverified, possibly exaggerated, and open to interpretation. Seeds player-driven exploration.
- **Quest Hook:** A confirmed problem posted publicly â€” on a notice board, announced by a crier, or declared at a temple.
- **Job:** A direct commission from a patron with a specific task and negotiated pay.

1. **Choose a category** â€” Rumor, Quest Hook, or Job.
2. **Set the party level** â€” determines reward scope, coin range, and treasure quality. Use "Random" for variety.
3. **Roll** â€” the generator fills all blanks from the tables below and calculates reward values.
4. **Add campaign detail** â€” replace abstract fragments with specific names, creatures, and places from your setting.

### Generating Multiple Leads

During a scene â€” an evening at an inn, a visit to the town square, or a meeting with a contact â€” generate several leads at once. Roll one of each category for three leads with different levels of detail and commitment. Mix tier brackets for variety:

- A **Regional job** that pays less but has a moral pull.

## Sentence Seeds

Each category uses a template sentence. The generator fills the blanks from the d66 tables below.

- **Rumor:** *"[Source] says that [rumor subject] near [location]. They say it's because of [cause]."*
- **Quest Hook:** *"Wanted: [task] [subject] at [location]. Warning: [complication]. Reward: [reward]."*
- **Job:** *"[Patron] wants you to [task] [subject] at [location], but [complication]. On success: [reward]."*
