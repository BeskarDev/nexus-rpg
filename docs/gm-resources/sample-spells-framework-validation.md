# Sample Spells Using Aspect Combination Framework

**Purpose**: This document contains sample spells created using the aspect combination framework to validate its effectiveness and provide concrete examples for spell designers.

---

## Validation Criteria

Each sample spell demonstrates:
1. **Aspect selection** from appropriate tradition/discipline
2. **Effect template** application
3. **Delivery method** consistency
4. **Rank-appropriate power**
5. **Clear mechanical implementation**
6. **Thematic coherence**

---

## Sample 1: Arcane Evocation Damage Spell

### **Thunderous Blast** (*evocation*, Rank 3)
**Properties:** blast (burst)

**Aspects Used**: *lightning, thunder, blast* (3 aspects)  
**Effect Template**: Direct Damage (AoE)  
**Delivery**: Blast (burst) - area effect  
**Power Level**: Rank 3 AoE = +6/12/18 damage

**Effect**
You create a crackling orb of lightning that explodes in a thunderous detonation, shocking all nearby creatures.
**Weak.** Deal +6 lightning damage to all creatures in close area.
**Strong.** Deal +12 lightning damage to all creatures in close area. Each target is briefly staggered by the concussive blast.
**Critical.** Deal +18 lightning damage to all creatures in close area. Each target is briefly stunned and pushed 1 range category away from the blast center.

**Validation**:
- ✅ Uses 3 Evocation aspects (*lightning, thunder, blast*)
- ✅ Damage matches Rank 3 AoE formula (+6/12/18)
- ✅ Close area appropriate for Rank 3
- ✅ Conditions (staggered/stunned) scale with success
- ✅ Blast shape clearly defined
- ✅ Power budget: 1 (damage) + 0.5 (condition on strong) + 0.5 (push on crit) = 2/4 ✅

## Sample 2: Mystic Life Healing Spell

### **Circle of Renewal** (*life*, Rank 2)
**Properties:** blast (aura), concentrate

**Aspects Used**: *vitality, restoration, community* (3 aspects)  
**Effect Template**: Buff/Enchantment + Zone  
**Delivery**: Blast (aura) - zone centered on caster  
**Power Level**: Rank 2 buff = +2 bonus or moderate healing

**Effect**
You create a luminous circle of healing energy that surrounds you and moves as you move, mending wounds and bolstering your allies.
**Weak.** Create a close area aura. Allies inside (including you) regenerate 2 HP at the start of their turns. Lasts short duration while you concentrate.
**Strong.** Create a close area aura. Allies regenerate 4 HP and gain +1 boon on Fortitude rolls. Lasts medium duration while you concentrate.
**Critical.** Create a short area aura. Allies regenerate 6 HP, gain +1 boon on Fortitude rolls, and are immune to disease and poison while inside. Lasts medium duration while you concentrate.

The aura moves with you and allies must remain inside to benefit.

**Validation**:
- ✅ Uses 3 Life aspects (*vitality, restoration, community*)
- ✅ Regeneration values appropriate (2/4/6 HP per turn)
- ✅ Close area standard for Rank 2
- ✅ Concentration required for mobile aura
- ✅ Scales area size and benefits with success
- ✅ Power budget: 1 (healing) + 0.5 (fortitude bonus) + 0.5 (immunity on crit) = 2/3 ✅

## Sample 3: Arcane Telepathy Control Spell

### **Mind Prison** (*telepathy*, Rank 4)
**Properties:** concentrate, singular

**Aspects Used**: *domination, mental-command* (2 aspects)  
**Effect Template**: Debuff/Condition  
**Delivery**: Single-target vs. Resist  
**Power Level**: Rank 4 severe condition with saves

**Effect**
You invade a creature's mind and trap their consciousness, leaving their body a helpless puppet under your control.
**Weak.** Target makes Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 10. On failure, target is dominated (charmed and obeys simple mental commands) for briefly while you concentrate. Target can save at end of each of their turns.
**Strong.** On failure, dominated for short duration. You can issue complex commands as a Quick Action. Target saves only at end of duration or when taking damage.
**Critical.** On failure, dominated for medium duration. You can see through their eyes and issue complex commands. Target can only save when taking damage, with +1 bane on the save.

While dominated, target won't take obviously suicidal actions. Breaking your concentration immediately ends the effect.

**Validation**:
- ✅ Uses 2 Telepathy aspects (*domination, mental-command*)
- ✅ TN 10 matches Rank 4 (6 + 4)
- ✅ Dominated is severe condition, appropriate for Rank 4
- ✅ Duration scales: briefly → short → medium
- ✅ Concentration required (powerful ongoing effect)
- ✅ Has counterplay (saves, damage breaks it)
- ✅ Power budget: 2 (severe condition) + 0.5 (scrying on crit) = 2.5/5 ✅

## Sample 4: Mystic Tempest Zone Spell

### **Eye of the Storm** (*tempest*, Rank 4)
**Properties:** blast (aura), concentrate

**Aspects Used**: *hurricane, wind, storm* (3 aspects)  
**Effect Template**: Zone/Area Control  
**Delivery**: Blast (aura) - mobile zone  
**Power Level**: Rank 4 zone with difficult terrain and damage

**Effect**
You become the center of a raging tempest, with howling winds and lashing rain swirling around you while you remain in the calm eye.
**Weak.** Create a close area storm centered on you. The area is difficult terrain and heavily obscured. Enemies that start their turn in the storm take +5 blast damage. Lasts short duration while you concentrate.
**Strong.** Create a short area storm. Difficult terrain, heavily obscured, +10 blast damage per turn. You can use a Quick Action to push one creature in the storm 1 range category. Lasts medium duration.
**Critical.** Create a medium area storm. Difficult terrain, heavily obscured, +15 blast damage per turn. All enemies in the storm are slowed, and you can push up to 3 creatures per turn. Lasts long duration.

The storm moves with you. Allies within melee range of you (in the eye) are unaffected by the storm.

**Validation**:
- ✅ Uses 3 Tempest aspects (*hurricane, wind, storm*)
- ✅ Area sizes scale appropriately (close → short → medium)
- ✅ Damage is half of Rank 4 single-target (+5/10/15)
- ✅ Concentration required for mobile zone
- ✅ Difficult terrain + obscurement + damage balanced
- ✅ "Eye" concept provides clear ally protection
- ✅ Power budget: 1 (zone) + 0.5 (damage) + 0.5 (push/control) = 2/5 ✅

## Sample 5: Arcane Conjuration Summoning Spell

### **Summon Shadow Assassin** (*conjuration*, Rank 4)
**Properties:** concentrate

**Aspects Used**: *summoning, creature, binding* (3 aspects)  
**Effect Template**: Summoning/Creation  
**Delivery**: Medium range summon point  
**Power Level**: Rank 4 summon = Tier 3-5 creatures

**Effect**
You tear open a rift to the shadow realm and forcibly summon a deadly assassin of living darkness to serve your will.
**Weak.** Summon one Tier 3 shadow assassin in medium range. It has Stealth (5) and deals necrotic damage with its attacks. Obeys your mental commands for short duration while you concentrate.
**Strong.** Summon one Tier 4 shadow assassin for medium duration. It can become invisible as a Quick Action once per turn.
**Critical.** Summon one Tier 5 shadow assassin or two Tier 4 shadow assassins for medium duration. They can teleport through shadows up to close range once per turn.

The assassin acts on your initiative. If your concentration breaks, it immediately turns hostile and attacks the nearest creature.

**Validation**:
- ✅ Uses 3 Conjuration aspects (*summoning, creature, binding*)
- ✅ Creature tiers match Rank 4 guidelines (3/4/5)
- ✅ Duration scales: short → medium → medium
- ✅ Concentration required (creatures act independently)
- ✅ Shadow theme integrated (stealth, invisibility, teleport)
- ✅ Hostile on break provides risk/counterplay
- ✅ Power budget: 1.5 (summon) + 0.5 (special abilities) = 2/5 ✅

## Sample 6: Mystic Death Debuff Spell

### **Withering Curse** (*death*, Rank 3)
**Properties:** -

**Aspects Used**: *curse, wither, decay* (3 aspects)  
**Effect Template**: Debuff/Condition  
**Delivery**: Single-target vs. Resist  
**Power Level**: Rank 3 moderate condition with attribute penalty

**Effect**
You place a curse of entropy upon your target, causing their flesh to wither and their strength to fade.
**Weak.** Target makes Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 9. On failure, target suffers -1 bane on Strength and Agility rolls for short duration.
**Strong.** On failure, target's Strength and Agility dice are reduced by one size and they take +2 damage from all sources for short duration.
**Critical.** On failure, both attribute dice reduced by one size, +4 damage taken from all sources, and target's maximum HP is reduced by 10 for medium duration. Reduced HP returns when curse ends.

The curse is a magical effect and can be dispelled.

**Validation**:
- ✅ Uses 3 Death aspects (*curse, wither, decay*)
- ✅ TN 9 matches Rank 3 (6 + 3)
- ✅ Conditions scale in severity
- ✅ Duration scales: short → short → medium
- ✅ Attribute reduction appropriate for Rank 3
- ✅ HP reduction on critical is significant but temporary
- ✅ Power budget: 1 (moderate condition) + 1 (attribute penalty) = 2/4 ✅

## Sample 7: Arcane Illusion Utility Spell

### **Phantom Refuge** (*illusion*, Rank 3)
**Properties:** illusory, concentrate

**Aspects Used**: *hallucination, false-terrain, misdirection* (3 aspects)  
**Effect Template**: Utility/Exploration + Zone  
**Delivery**: Medium range, creates structure  
**Power Level**: Rank 3 utility with tactical benefits

**Effect**
You create a detailed illusion of a small structure—such as a cottage, tent, or cave entrance—that can provide concealment and mislead pursuers.
**Weak.** Create an illusory structure up to close area in size. Creatures can enter and hide inside. Those inside are heavily obscured from outside. Lasts medium duration while you concentrate.
**Strong.** As weak, but the structure can also include convincing sounds (crackling fire, snoring, etc.) and those inside gain +2 boons on Stealth rolls. The structure can be placed up to short area away.
**Critical.** As strong, but the illusion is perfect and includes tactile elements (illusory furniture, warmth, smells). Creatures must succeed on Spirit + Perception vs. your Resist to detect the illusion even when interacting with it. The structure can be placed up to medium range away.

Creatures that disbelieve the illusion can pass through it freely. Physical attacks pass through the walls.

**Validation**:
- ✅ Uses 3 Illusion aspects (*hallucination, false-terrain, misdirection*)
- ✅ Utility-focused with tactical benefits
- ✅ Area size appropriate for Rank 3 (close → short → medium)
- ✅ Concentration required for maintaining complex illusion
- ✅ Illusory property allows saves to disbelieve
- ✅ Scales in convincingness and utility
- ✅ Power budget: 1 (utility) + 0.5 (stealth bonus) = 1.5/4 ✅

## Sample 8: Mystic Peace Defensive Spell

### **Sanctuary of Law** (*peace*, Rank 3)
**Properties:** blast (aura), concentrate

**Aspects Used**: *sanctuary, protection, law* (3 aspects)  
**Effect Template**: Zone/Area Control + Buff  
**Delivery**: Blast (aura) - zone centered on location  
**Power Level**: Rank 3 zone with protective effects

**Effect**
You consecrate an area with the power of peace and order, creating a sanctuary that prevents violence and protects those within.
**Weak.** Create a close area sanctuary. Hostile creatures that attempt to attack or cast harmful spells while inside suffer +2 banes on the roll. Lasts medium duration while you concentrate.
**Strong.** Create a short area sanctuary. Hostile creatures suffer +2 banes and allies inside gain +2 AV. Additionally, loud noise and violence are suppressed (sounds are muffled, bright flashes dimmed). Lasts medium duration.
**Critical.** Create a medium area sanctuary. Hostile creatures suffer +3 banes and allies gain +2 AV and +1 boon on Resist. The area is considered consecrated ground for magical purposes. Lasts long duration.

The sanctuary is a visible aura of soft golden light. Allies can freely attack from inside but lose benefits if they make hostile actions.

**Validation**:
- ✅ Uses 3 Peace aspects (*sanctuary, protection, law*)
- ✅ Area sizes scale: close → short → medium
- ✅ +2 AV matches Rank 3 defensive bonus
- ✅ Concentration appropriate for powerful effect
- ✅ Bane penalty scales with success (2 → 2 → 3)
- ✅ Clear limitations (allies lose benefits if attacking)
- ✅ Power budget: 1 (zone) + 1 (defensive bonuses) = 2/4 ✅

## Sample 9: Arcane Necromancy Damage + Healing Hybrid

### **Vampiric Drain** (*necromancy*, Rank 3)
**Properties:** -

**Aspects Used**: *life-drain, siphoning* (2 aspects)  
**Effect Template**: Direct Damage + Self-Healing (Hybrid)  
**Delivery**: Single-target vs. Dodge  
**Power Level**: Rank 3 reduced damage due to healing

**Effect**
You draw forth the life essence of your target in a visible stream of dark energy, siphoning their vitality to restore your own.
**Weak.** Deal +6 necrotic damage (reduced from +8). You heal HP equal to half the damage dealt.
**Strong.** Deal +12 necrotic damage (reduced from +16). You heal HP equal to half the damage dealt and gain +1 boon on your next attack roll.
**Critical.** Deal +18 necrotic damage (reduced from +24). You heal HP equal to the full damage dealt and the target is briefly weakened (-2 banes on Strength rolls).

This spell deals no damage to undead or constructs, and you gain no healing if the target is immune to necrotic damage.

**Validation**:
- ✅ Uses 2 Necromancy aspects (*life-drain, siphoning*)
- ✅ Damage reduced by 1 step due to healing (+6/12/18 vs +8/16/24)
- ✅ Healing scales: half → half → full
- ✅ Brief weakened condition appropriate
- ✅ Clear limitations (undead/construct immunity)
- ✅ Power budget: 1 (reduced damage) + 1 (healing) + 0.5 (weak condition) = 2.5/4 ✅

## Sample 10: Mystic War Buff Spell

### **Warlord's Command** (*war*, Rank 2)
**Properties:** quick

**Aspects Used**: *tactical-advantage, battle-cry, martial-prowess* (3 aspects)  
**Effect Template**: Buff/Enchantment (Multi-target)  
**Delivery**: Quick Action, close range allies  
**Power Level**: Rank 2 tactical buff

**Effect**
You shout a commanding battle cry that rallies your allies and coordinates their assault.
**Weak.** Up to 3 allies in close range gain +1 boon on their next attack roll. Allies must use this benefit before end of next turn.
**Strong.** Up to 3 allies gain +1 boon on attack rolls and +2 damage for briefly (end of next turn).
**Critical.** Up to 5 allies gain +1 boon on attack rolls, +4 damage, and can immediately move up to 1 range category as a reaction. Benefits last until end of next turn.

Cast this spell as a Quick Action on your turn or as a reaction when an ally is attacked.

**Validation**:
- ✅ Uses 3 War aspects (*tactical-advantage, battle-cry, martial-prowess*)
- ✅ Quick Action property allows tactical use
- ✅ +1 boon and damage bonuses match Rank 2
- ✅ Target quantity scales (3 → 3 → 5)
- ✅ Brief duration appropriate for combat bonus
- ✅ Reactive casting adds tactical depth
- ✅ Power budget: 0.5 (boon) + 0.5 (damage on strong) + 0.5 (movement on crit) = 1.5/3 ✅
