# Tier 8

Tier 8 creatures are among the most dangerous beings. They include ancient dragons, undying sorcerer-kings, and infernal nobility.

---

### **Ancient Fire Dragon** (Gargantuan Draconic)

**Tier:** 8 (Lord)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 3x80 | 16 (natural heavy) | d12+1 | d8 | d12 | d10 | 16 | 12 | 14 |

**Skills:** Athletics (5), Fighting (5), Fortitude (5), Insight (5), Lore (5), Perception (5), Survival (5)

**Immunities:** fire damage, frightened

**Attacks:**

- **2 x Claw** (*light, slash*). 15/27/39 damage.
- **Bite** (*crush, reach*). 18/33/48 damage. On a strong or critical hit and if the target is smaller than this creature, the target is grappled and restrained.
- **Tail** (*crush, reach*). 15/27/39 damage. On a strong hit, the target is pushed close. On a critical hit, the target is pushed close and falls prone.
- **Dragon's Breath** (*recharge (d6)*). Roll Strength + Fortitude vs. Dodge. Target all creatures in long range in a cone. On a success, each target suffers 30 fire damage. On a strong or critical success, each target also suffers burning (10).

**Abilities:**

- **Flying (Passive).** This creature can fly with 2 Movement per turn. If on the ground, it can only spend 1 Movement during its turn in total.
- **Legendary Resistance (Passive, 3/day).** If this creature fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Frightful Presence (Action).** Each creature of this creature's choice within very long range must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 14 or become frightened for a short duration. A creature can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns.
- **Infernal Awakening (Lord Trigger).** When this creature loses a life pool, fire erupts from its scales. All areas within short range become burning terrain (dealing 6 fire damage to any creature that enters or starts its turn there) for the rest of the scene.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Wing Attack.** This creature beats its wings. Each creature within close range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 14 or take 12 damage and be knocked prone. This creature can then fly up to half its flying speed.
- **Tail Sweep.** This creature sweeps its tail. Each creature within close range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 14 or take 12 damage and be pushed close.
- **Detect.** This creature makes a Perception check.

### **Lich** (Medium Undead)

**Tier:** 8 (Lord)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 3x80 | 9 (natural light + arcane ward) | d8 | d10 | d10 | d12+1 | 13 | 14 | 15 |

**Skills:** Arcana (5), Education (5), Fighting (4), Fortitude (5), Insight (5), Lore (5), Perception (5)

**Resistances:** cold, lightning, necrotic damage

**Immunities:** poison damage, bleeding, charmed, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, unconscious

**Attacks:**

- **Paralyzing Touch** (*target: vs. Resist*). 14/24/34 cold damage. The target must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 14 or be paralyzed for a short duration.
- **Disintegrate** (*range (long), recharge (d6)*). 20/36/52 force damage. If this damage reduces the target to 0 HP, the target is disintegrated.

**Abilities:**

- **Undead Nature (Passive).** This creature is immune to bleeding, being charmed, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, or falling unconscious.
- **Legendary Resistance (Passive, 3/day).** If this creature fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Rejuvenation (Passive).** If this creature has a phylactery, it reforms next to its phylactery in 1d10 days if it is destroyed.
- **Innate Spellcasting (Passive).** This creature can cast the following spells rolling Mind + Arcana once per scene each: *Animate Dead*, *Cloudkill*, *Counterspell*, *Dimension Door*, *Dispel Magic*, *Fireball*, *Globe of Invulnerability*, *Hold Monster*, *Power Word Stun*.
- **Soul Harvest (Lord Trigger).** When this creature loses a life pool, all living creatures within short range must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 14 or take 10 necrotic damage and have their maximum HP reduced by that amount until they finish a long rest. This creature heals HP equal to the total damage dealt.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Shield.** This creature can cast a magical shield, gaining +5 Dodge until the start of its next turn.
- **Frightening Gaze.** This creature targets one creature within short range. The target must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 14 or be frightened until the end of their next turn.

### **Storm Giant King** (Huge Giant)

**Tier:** 8 (Elite)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 2x80 | 16 (heavy) | d12+1 | d10 | d12 | d10 | 16 | 13 | 14 |

**Skills:** Athletics (5), Fighting (5), Fortitude (5), Insight (5), Perception (5)

**Immunities:** lightning, thunder damage

**Attacks:**

- **Greatsword** (*heavy (d12), slash, two-handed*). 18/33/48 damage plus 6 lightning damage.
- **Rock** (*range (very long), crush*). 17/31/45 damage.

**Abilities:**

- **Amphibious (Passive).** This creature can breathe air and water.
- **Innate Spellcasting (Passive).** This creature can cast the following spells once per scene each: *Control Weather*, *Call Lightning*, *Feather Fall*, *Levitate*, *Water Breathing*.
- **Lightning Strike (Action, recharge (d6)).** This creature calls down a bolt of lightning at a point it can see within long range. Each creature within close range of that point must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 14 or take 18 lightning damage and be stunned until the end of their next turn.
- **Thunderous Fury (Elite Trigger).** When this creature loses its first life pool, a thunderstorm erupts around it. All creatures within medium range must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 14 or take 12 thunder damage and be deafened for a short duration.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Thunder Step.** This creature disappears in a clap of thunder and reappears in a space it can see within short range. Each creature within melee range of its original location takes 6 thunder damage.

### **Archfiend** (Large Spirit)

**Tier:** 8 (Elite)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 2x80 | 16 (natural heavy) | d12+1 | d10 | d12 | d12 | 15 | 13 | 14 |

**Skills:** Arcana (5), Fighting (5), Fortitude (5), Insight (5), Perception (5), Influence (5)

**Resistances:** cold damage, physical damage (from non-magical weapons)

**Immunities:** fire, poison damage, poisoned

**Attacks:**

- **Bite** (*crush*). 16/29/42 damage. The target must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 14 or be poisoned for a short duration.
- **Claw** (*slash*). 14/25/36 damage.
- **Burning Mace** (*crush*). 16/29/42 damage plus 8 fire damage.
- **Tail** (*reach*). 14/25/36 damage.

**Abilities:**

- **Fear Aura (Passive).** Any creature hostile to this creature that starts its turn within short range must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 14 or be frightened until the start of its next turn.
- **Magic Resistance (Passive).** This creature has +1 boon on saves against spells and other magical effects.
- **Innate Spellcasting (Passive).** This creature can cast the following spells once per scene each: *Detect Magic*, *Fireball*, *Hold Monster*, *Wall of Fire*.
- **Infernal Reinforcements (Elite Trigger).** When this creature loses its first life pool, it summons 1d4 Hell Hounds which appear within short range and act on this creature's initiative.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Infernal Wound.** When this creature hits with a melee attack, the target takes an additional 8 fire damage and must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 14 or lose 4 HP at the start of each of its turns due to an infernal wound.

### **Kraken** (Gargantuan Monstrosity)

**Tier:** 8 (Lord)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 3x80 | 16 (natural heavy) | d12+2 | d8 | d10 | d10 | 17 | 11 | 14 |

**Skills:** Athletics (5), Fighting (5), Perception (5)

**Immunities:** lightning damage, frightened, paralyzed

**Attacks:**

- **Tentacle** (*reach*). 15/27/39 damage. On a hit, the target is grappled and restrained. This creature has ten tentacles, each of which can grapple one target.
- **Bite** (*crush, only on grappled targets*). 18/33/48 damage plus 6 lightning damage.
- **Lightning Storm** (*recharge (d6)*). Roll Strength + Fortitude vs. Dodge. Three bolts of lightning strike points this creature can see within long range. Each creature within close range of a bolt must roll. On a failure, the creature takes 16 lightning damage, or half on a success.

**Abilities:**

- **Amphibious (Passive).** This creature can breathe air and water.
- **Freedom of Movement (Passive).** This creature ignores difficult terrain, and magical effects can't reduce its speed or cause it to be restrained.
- **Siege Monster (Passive).** This creature deals double damage to objects and structures.
- **Ink Cloud (Action, 1/day).** While underwater, this creature expels ink in a large area. All creatures in the ink cloud are blinded while inside it. The cloud lasts for a short duration.
- **Crushing Depths (Lord Trigger).** When this creature loses a life pool, the water pressure around it intensifies. All creatures within short range must roll Strength + Fortitude vs. TN 14 or take 12 damage and be unable to move until the end of their next turn.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Fling.** One creature grappled by this creature is thrown up to medium range. The creature takes 10 damage and lands prone.
- **Tentacle Strike.** This creature makes one tentacle attack as a Quick Action.

**Immunities:** fire damage, burning, frightened

- **Talons** (*slash*). 14/25/36 fire damage.
- **Beak** (*pierce*). 15/27/39 fire damage.

**Abilities:**

- **Fire Form (Passive).** A creature that touches this creature or hits it with a melee attack while within melee range takes 10 fire damage.
