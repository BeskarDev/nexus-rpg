# Tier 2

Tier 2 creatures are slightly more dangerous, including stronger undead, minor magical beings, and organized humanoid fighters.

---

### **Zombie** (Medium Undead)

**Tier:** 2 (Basic)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 20 | 2 (natural light) | d8 | d4 | d4 | d4-1 | 8 | 6 | 8 |

**Skills:** Fortitude (2), Fighting (1)

**Weaknesses:** slash weapons, radiant damage

**Resistances:** pierce weapons, necrotic damage

**Immunities:** bleeding, charmed, confused, deafened, frightened, poisoned, unconscious

**Attacks:**

- **Claw**. 6/10/14 damage. On a hit, attempts to grapple the target.
- **Rotten Bite** (*only if grappling*). 8/14/20 poison damage.

**Abilities:**

- **Undead Nature (Passive).** This creature is immune to bleeding, being charmed, confused, deafened, frightened, poisoned, or falling unconscious.
- **Rotting Slowness (Passive).** This creature can only move or take an Action per turn, not both. This creature can't use Quick Actions.

### **Medium Fire Elemental** (Medium Spirit)

**Tier:** 2 (Basic)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 20 | 2 (natural light) | d8 | d8 | d8 | d6 | 8 | 9 | 8 |

**Skills:** Fighting (2), Fortitude (2), Perception (2)

**Weaknesses:** frost damage, damage from water

**Resistances:** physical damage

**Immunities:** fire damage, burning, bleeding, poisoned, grappled, restrained, stunned, unconscious

**Attacks:**

- **Fast Strikes.** This creature can attack or cast spells twice per turn.
- **Burning Lash**. 6/10/14 fire damage. On a strong or critical hit, inflicts burning (4) for a short duration.
- **Flame Bolt** (*range (medium)*). 6/10/14 fire damage. On a strong or critical hit, inflicts burning (2) for a short duration.

**Abilities:**

- **Ethereal Form (Passive).** This creature is immune to bleeding, poison damage, being poisoned, being grappled, restrained, stunned, or falling unconscious.
- **Flame Body (Passive).** This creature is immune to fire damage and burning. This creature illuminates the area in short distance around it in bright light. If this creature is hit by an attack at melee range, the attacker suffers 4 fire damage (ignoring AV). If this creature comes into contact with a large amount of water, it takes 5 damage (ignoring AV) each turn doing so.

### **Goblin Boss** (Small Humanoid)

**Tier:** 2 (Lord)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 3x20 | 3 (light) | d4 | d8 | d4 | d6 | 7 | 9 | 8 |

**Skills:** Archery (1), Athletics (2), Fighting (2), Perception (2), Stealth (2)

**Attacks:**

- **Fast Strikes.** This creature can attack twice per turn.
- **Scimitar** (*agile, light, slash*). 6/10/14 damage.
- **Whip** (*agile, entangle, light, reach, slash*). 6/10/14 damage.
- **Dart** (*agile, bundle, light, pierce, thrown (short)*). 6/10/14 damage.

**Abilities:**

- **Night Vision (Passive).** This creature can see one range category further from sources of bright and dim light.
- **Rally Goblin (Action).** On this creature's turn, it can target one goblin visible in medium range. That goblin immediately makes an attack against one target of this creature's choice. The goblin gains +1 boon on the attack. This ability can be used once per turn.
- **Quick Escape (Passive).** When this creature was missed by a melee attack, it can move close.
- **Goblin Reinforcements (Lord Trigger).** When this creature loses a life pool, it summons 1d4+2 goblins, which appear at any location in short range of this creature.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Sacrifice Goblin.** When this creature would be hit by an attack, it can redirect the attack to target one goblin close to them.
