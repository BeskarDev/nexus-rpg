# Tier 6

Tier 6 creatures are fearsome opponents. They include powerful undead lords, legendary beasts, and master combatants.

---

### **Adult Fire Dragon** (Huge Draconic)

**Tier:** 6 (Elite)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 2x60 | 12 (natural heavy) | d12 | d8 | d10 | d10 | 14 | 10 | 12 |

**Skills:** Athletics (4), Fighting (4), Fortitude (4), Insight (4), Lore (4), Perception (4), Survival (4)

**Resistances:** fire damage

**Immunities:** frightened

**Attacks:**

- **2 x Claw** (*light, slash*). 12/21/30 damage.
- **Bite** (*crush, reach*). 15/27/39 damage. On a strong or critical hit and if the target is smaller than this creature, the target is grappled and restrained.
- **Tail** (*crush, reach*). 12/21/30 damage. On a strong hit, the target is pushed close. On a critical hit, the target is pushed close and falls prone.
- **Dragon's Breath** (*recharge (d6)*). Roll Strength + Fortitude vs. Dodge. Target all creatures in medium range in a cone. On a success, each target suffers 24 fire damage. On a strong or critical success, each target also suffers burning (8).

**Abilities:**

- **Flying (Passive).** This creature can fly with 2 Movement per turn. If on the ground, it can only spend 1 Movement during its turn in total.
- **Legendary Resistance (Passive, 2/day).** If this creature fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Fiery Wrath (Elite Trigger).** When this creature loses its first life pool, it immediately regains its Dragon's Breath and uses it.
- **Frightful Presence (Action).** Each creature of this creature's choice within long range must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 12 or become frightened for a short duration. A creature can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Wing Attack.** This creature beats its wings. Each creature within close range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 12 or take 10 damage and be knocked prone. This creature can then fly up to half its flying speed.
- **Tail Sweep.** This creature sweeps its tail. Each creature within close range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 12 or take 10 damage and be pushed close.

### **Vampire Lord** (Medium Undead)

**Tier:** 6 (Lord)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 3x60 | 7 (natural light) | d12 | d10 | d10 | d10 | 12 | 12 | 12 |

**Skills:** Arcana (4), Athletics (4), Fighting (4), Fortitude (4), Influence (4), Insight (4), Perception (4), Stealth (4)

**Weaknesses:** radiant damage, running water (20 damage when ending turn in it)

**Resistances:** necrotic damage, physical damage (from non-magical weapons)

**Immunities:** bleeding, charmed, frightened, poisoned, unconscious

**Attacks:**

- **Unarmed Strike**. 12/21/30 damage.
- **Bite** (*only on grappled, incapacitated, restrained, or willing targets*). 14/25/36 necrotic damage. The target's maximum HP is reduced by the damage dealt until they finish a long rest, and this creature regains HP equal to that amount.

**Abilities:**

- **Undead Nature (Passive).** This creature is immune to bleeding, being charmed, frightened, poisoned, or falling unconscious.
- **Regeneration (Passive).** This creature regains 15 HP at the start of its turn if it has at least 1 HP and isn't in sunlight or running water.
- **Spider Climb (Passive).** This creature can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings.
- **Charm (Action).** This creature targets one humanoid within short range. The target must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 12 or be charmed.
- **Children of the Night (Action, 1/day).** This creature calls 3d6 wolves or 2d6 vampire spawn. They arrive in 1d4 rounds.
- **Blood Frenzy (Lord Trigger).** When this creature loses a life pool, all creatures within short range must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 12 or be frightened until the end of their next turn. This creature can immediately make a Bite attack against a creature within melee range.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Misty Escape.** When this creature drops to 0 HP, it can transform into a cloud of mist and move up to medium range. It must reach its resting place within 2 hours or be destroyed.

**Tier:** 6 (Basic)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 60 | 12 (heavy) | d12 | d6 | d10 | d8 | 14 | 10 | 12 |

**Skills:** Athletics (4), Fighting (4), Fortitude (4), Perception (4)

**Attacks:**

- **Rock** (*range (very long), crush*). 14/25/36 damage.
- **Flaming Boulder** (*range (very long), crush*). 14/25/36 fire damage. On a hit, the area becomes burning terrain for a short duration.

**Abilities:**

- **Fire Aura (Passive).** At the start of this creature's turn, each creature within melee range takes 4 fire damage.
- **Heated Weapons (Passive).** This creature's metal weapons deal an extra 4 fire damage (included above).

**Quick Actions:**

- **Stomp.** One creature within melee range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 12 or take 8 damage and fall prone.

### **Hydra** (Huge Monstrosity)

**Tier:** 6 (Basic)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 60 | 7 (natural light) | d12 | d6 | d8 | d4 | 13 | 10 | 12 |

**Skills:** Fighting (4), Fortitude (4), Perception (4)

**Attacks:**

- **Bite** (*reach*). 12/21/30 damage. This creature makes one bite attack for each of its heads.

**Abilities:**

- **Hold Breath (Passive).** This creature can hold its breath for one hour.
- **Multiple Heads (Passive).** This creature has five heads. While it has more than one head, it gains +1 boon on saves against being blinded, charmed, deafened, frightened, stunned, and knocked unconscious. Whenever this creature takes 25 or more damage in a single turn, one of its heads dies.
- **Reactive Heads (Passive).** For each head this creature has beyond one, it gets an extra Quick Action that it can use only for opportunity attacks.
- **Head Regrowth (Passive).** This creature regrows two heads for each of its heads that dies unless it takes fire or acid damage before the start of its next turn. For each head regrown in this way, this creature regains 10 HP.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Opportunity Attack.** When a creature within reach moves away without disengaging, this creature can make one bite attack against it.

### **Sphinx** (Large Monstrosity)

**Tier:** 6 (Elite)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 2x60 | 12 (natural heavy) | d10 | d10 | d12 | d12 | 13 | 12 | 13 |

**Skills:** Arcana (4), Education (4), Fighting (4), Insight (4), Lore (4), Perception (4)

**Immunities:** psychic damage, charmed, frightened

**Resistances:** physical damage (from non-magical weapons)

**Attacks:**

- **2 x Claw** (*light, slash*). 11/19/27 damage.
- **Bite** (*crush*). 13/23/33 damage.

**Abilities:**

- **Flying (Passive).** This creature can fly with 2 Movement per turn. If on the ground, it can only spend 1 Movement during its turn in total.
- **Inscrutable (Passive).** This creature is immune to any effect that would sense its emotions or read its thoughts, and Insight checks to determine its intentions or sincerity have +2 banes.
- **Innate Spellcasting (Passive).** This creature can cast the following spells rolling Mind + Arcana once per scene each: *Dispel Magic*, *Hold Person*, *Legend Lore*, *Locate Object*, *Zone of Truth*, *Banishment*.
- **Legendary Resistance (Passive, 2/day).** If this creature fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Riddle of the Ages (Elite Trigger).** When this creature loses its first life pool, it poses a magical riddle. All enemies within medium range must roll Mind + Education vs. TN 12 or be confused until the end of their next turn as their minds struggle with impossible knowledge.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Roar.** All creatures within short range must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 12 or be frightened until the end of their next turn.
- **Time Manipulation.** This creature can age or de-age one creature within short range by up to 1d10 years (Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 12 negates).

**Weaknesses:** radiant damage

**Resistances:** acid, cold, fire, lightning, necrotic damage, physical damage (from non-magical weapons)

**Attacks:**

- **Life Drain** (*target: vs. Resist*). 12/21/30 necrotic damage. The target must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 12 or have its maximum HP reduced by the damage dealt until it finishes a long rest.

**Abilities:**

- **Undead Nature (Passive).** This creature is immune to bleeding, being charmed, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, or falling unconscious.

- **Create Specter (Action).** This creature targets a humanoid that died within the last minute from this creature's Life Drain. The target's spirit rises as a specter under this creature's control.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Shadow Step.** While in dim light or darkness, this creature teleports up to close range to another area of dim light or darkness.
