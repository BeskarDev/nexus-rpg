# Tier 9

Tier 9 creatures represent near-mythical threats. They include the oldest dragons, elemental lords, and transcendent undead.

---

### **Primordial Wyrm** (Colossal Draconic)

**Tier:** 9 (Lord)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 3x90 | 18 (natural heavy) | d12+1 | d8 | d12 | d12 | 17 | 13 | 15 |

**Skills:** Arcana (5), Athletics (5), Fighting (5), Fortitude (5), Insight (5), Lore (5), Perception (5), Survival (5)

**Immunities:** fire damage, frightened, charmed

**Attacks:**

- **2 x Claw** (*light, slash*). 17/31/45 damage.
- **Bite** (*crush, reach*). 20/37/54 damage. On a strong or critical hit and if the target is smaller than this creature, the target is grappled and restrained.
- **Tail** (*crush, reach*). 17/31/45 damage. On a strong hit, the target is pushed a short distance. On a critical hit, the target is pushed a short distance and falls prone.
- **Dragon's Breath** (*recharge (d4)*). Roll Strength + Fortitude vs. Dodge. Target all creatures in very long range in a cone. On a success, each target suffers 36 fire damage. On a strong or critical success, each target also suffers burning (12).

**Abilities:**

- **Flying (Passive).** This creature can fly with 3 Movement per turn. If on the ground, it can only spend 2 Movement during its turn in total.
- **Legendary Resistance (Passive, 3/day).** If this creature fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Magic Resistance (Passive).** This creature has +1 boon on saves against spells and other magical effects.
- **Frightful Presence (Action).** Each creature of this creature's choice within extreme range must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 15 or become frightened for a short duration. A creature can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns.
- **Innate Spellcasting (Passive).** This creature can cast the following spells once per scene each: *Dispel Magic*, *Fireball*, *Wall of Fire*, *Meteor Swarm*.
- **World Shaper (Lord Trigger).** When this creature loses a life pool, the terrain within long range transforms. Choose one: (1) Lava pools erupt, creating difficult terrain that deals 10 fire damage to any creature entering or starting its turn there; (2) Fire tornadoes appear, randomly moving and dealing 15 fire damage to creatures in their path; (3) The air becomes superheated, causing all creatures except this one to take 5 fire damage at the start of their turns.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Wing Attack.** This creature beats its wings. Each creature within short range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 15 or take 14 damage and be knocked prone. This creature can then fly up to half its flying speed.
- **Tail Sweep.** This creature sweeps its tail. Each creature within short range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 15 or take 14 damage and be pushed close.
- **Detect.** This creature makes a Perception check.

### **Primordial Fire Lord** (Huge Spirit)

**Tier:** 9 (Lord)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 3x90 | 18 (natural heavy) | d12+1 | d10 | d12+1 | d10 | 16 | 14 | 15 |

**Skills:** Arcana (5), Fighting (5), Fortitude (5), Insight (5), Perception (5)

**Immunities:** fire, poison damage, burning, poisoned, grappled, restrained, unconscious

**Resistances:** physical damage

**Attacks:**

- **Flame Sword** (*slash*). 18/33/48 fire damage.
- **Flame Wave** (*short cone*). 16/29/42 fire damage to all creatures in the area. On a strong or critical hit, targets suffer burning (8).
- **Meteor Strike** (*range (very long), recharge (d6)*). A ball of fire crashes down at a point this creature can see. All creatures within close range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 15 or take 24 fire damage and be knocked prone.

**Abilities:**

- **Fire Form (Passive).** This creature can move through a space as narrow as a crack without squeezing. A creature that touches this creature or hits it with a melee attack while within melee range takes 8 fire damage.
- **Illumination (Passive).** This creature sheds bright light in medium range and dim light for an additional medium range.
- **Legendary Resistance (Passive, 3/day).** If this creature fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Innate Spellcasting (Passive).** This creature can cast the following spells once per scene each: *Fireball*, *Wall of Fire*, *Fire Storm*, *Plane Shift*.
- **Conflagration (Lord Trigger).** When this creature loses a life pool, it explodes in a burst of flame. All creatures within medium range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 15 or take 18 fire damage. The area remains on fire for a short duration, dealing 8 fire damage to creatures that enter or start their turn there.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Flame Shield.** This creature surrounds itself with protective flames. Until the start of its next turn, it has +3 AV and any creature that hits it with a melee attack takes 8 fire damage.

### **Archlich** (Medium Undead)

**Tier:** 9 (Lord)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 3x90 | 10 (natural light + arcane ward) | d8 | d10 | d12 | d12+1 | 14 | 15 | 16 |

**Skills:** Arcana (5), Education (5), Fighting (5), Fortitude (5), Insight (5), Lore (5), Perception (5)

**Resistances:** cold, lightning, necrotic damage

**Immunities:** poison damage, bleeding, charmed, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, unconscious

**Attacks:**

- **Paralyzing Touch** (*target: vs. Resist*). 16/29/42 cold damage. The target must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 15 or be paralyzed for a short duration.
- **Soul Bolt** (*range (very long)*). 18/33/48 necrotic damage. On a strong or critical hit, the target's maximum HP is reduced by half the damage dealt.

**Abilities:**

- **Undead Nature (Passive).** This creature is immune to bleeding, being charmed, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, or falling unconscious.
- **Legendary Resistance (Passive, 3/day).** If this creature fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Rejuvenation (Passive).** If this creature has a phylactery, it reforms next to its phylactery in 1d10 days if it is destroyed.
- **Innate Spellcasting (Passive).** This creature can cast the following spells rolling Mind + Arcana once per scene each: *Animate Dead*, *Cloudkill*, *Counterspell*, *Dimension Door*, *Disintegrate*, *Dispel Magic*, *Dominate Person*, *Finger of Death*, *Globe of Invulnerability*, *Meteor Swarm*, *Power Word Kill*, *Time Stop*.
- **Dread Mastery (Lord Trigger).** When this creature loses a life pool, it unleashes ancient magic. Choose one: (1) All undead within long range heal 20 HP and gain +2 to all attack rolls; (2) All living creatures within medium range must save vs. TN 15 or be paralyzed until the end of their next turn; (3) This creature immediately casts two spells.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Shield.** This creature can cast a magical shield, gaining +5 Dodge until the start of its next turn.
- **Counterspell.** This creature can attempt to interrupt a spell being cast within short range.

### **Elder Primordial** (Gargantuan Spirit)

**Tier:** 9 (Elite)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 2x90 | 18 (natural heavy) | d12+1 | d6 | d12 | d8 | 17 | 12 | 15 |

**Skills:** Fighting (5), Fortitude (5), Perception (5)

**Immunities:** poison damage, poisoned, prone

**Resistances:** physical damage

**Attacks:**

- **Slam** (*reach*). 18/33/48 damage.
- **Engulf** (*only when touching*). The target must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 15 or be engulfed. While engulfed, the target is restrained, blinded, and takes 12 damage at the start of each of this creature's turns. This creature can engulf up to three creatures at once.

**Abilities:**

- **Elemental Nature (Passive).** This creature doesn't require air, food, drink, or sleep.
- **Siege Monster (Passive).** This creature deals double damage to objects and structures.
- **Legendary Resistance (Passive, 2/day).** If this creature fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Primordial Surge (Elite Trigger).** When this creature loses its first life pool, its size increases to Colossal and it gains +2 to all attack rolls and damage for the rest of the scene.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Earth Tremor.** All creatures within close range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 15 or fall prone.

### **Jotun Allfather** (Colossal Giant)

**Tier:** 9 (Lord)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 3x90 | 18 (natural heavy) | d12+2 | d6 | d12 | d10 | 17 | 12 | 15 |

**Skills:** Athletics (5), Fighting (5), Fortitude (5), Insight (5), Lore (5), Perception (5)

**Immunities:** cold damage, frightened

**Resistances:** fire, lightning damage

**Attacks:**

- **Primordial Greataxe** (*heavy (d12), slash, two-handed*). 20/37/54 damage plus 10 cold damage.
- **Ice Boulder** (*range (extreme), crush*). 18/33/48 cold damage. On a hit, the area becomes frozen difficult terrain.

**Abilities:**

- **Primordial Cold (Passive).** At the start of this creature's turn, each creature within melee range takes 8 cold damage.
- **Legendary Resistance (Passive, 3/day).** If this creature fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Innate Spellcasting (Passive).** This creature can cast the following spells once per scene each: *Control Weather*, *Cone of Cold*, *Ice Storm*, *Wall of Ice*.
- **Winter's End (Lord Trigger).** When this creature loses a life pool, a blizzard erupts around it. All areas within long range become frozen difficult terrain and heavily obscured. Any creature that starts its turn in the area takes 8 cold damage and must roll Strength + Fortitude vs. TN 15 or be slowed until the end of its turn.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Stomp.** All creatures within close range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 15 or take 12 damage and fall prone.
- **Ice Breath.** All creatures in a short cone must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 15 or take 14 cold damage and be slowed until the end of their next turn.

### **Apophis Serpent** (Colossal Monstrosity)

**Tier:** 9 (Lord)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 3x90 | 18 (natural heavy) | d12+2 | d8 | d10 | d8 | 17 | 12 | 15 |

**Skills:** Athletics (5), Fighting (5), Fortitude (5), Perception (5), Stealth (5)

**Immunities:** poison damage, poisoned, frightened

**Resistances:** necrotic damage

**Attacks:**

- **Bite** (*crush, reach*). 19/35/51 damage plus 8 poison damage. On a strong or critical hit, the target is swallowed if it is Huge or smaller. A swallowed creature is blinded and restrained, and takes 15 acid damage at the start of each of this creature's turns.
- **Constrict** (*crush*). 18/33/48 damage. On a hit, a creature up to Gargantuan size is grappled and restrained.
- **Tail** (*reach*). 17/31/45 damage. On a strong or critical hit, the target is knocked prone.

**Abilities:**

- **Darkness Incarnate (Passive).** This creature is wreathed in supernatural darkness. All light sources within medium range are suppressed, and attacks made against this creature from more than close range have +1 bane.
- **Legendary Resistance (Passive, 3/day).** If this creature fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Swallow the Sun (Lord Trigger).** When this creature loses a life pool, it can swallow one source of light (magical or mundane) within medium range. All creatures within long range must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 15 or be blinded for a short duration. The area remains in supernatural darkness for the rest of the scene.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Tail Sweep.** All creatures within close range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 15 or take 12 damage and be pushed close.
- **Venomous Spit.** One creature within medium range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 15 or take 10 poison damage and be poisoned until the end of their next turn.
