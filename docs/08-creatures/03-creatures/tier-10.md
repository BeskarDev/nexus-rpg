# Tier 10

Tier 10 creatures are the pinnacle of mortal-scale threats - legendary beings that reshape the world around them. Encounters with these creatures are campaign-defining events.

---

### **Tarrasque** (Colossal Monstrosity)

**Tier:** 10 (Lord)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 3x100 | 20 (natural heavy) | d12+2 | d8 | d10 | d4 | 18 | 12 | 16 |

**Skills:** Athletics (5), Fighting (5), Fortitude (5), Perception (5)

**Immunities:** fire, poison damage, charmed, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned

**Resistances:** physical damage (from non-magical weapons)

**Attacks:**

- **Bite** (*crush, reach*). 22/41/60 damage. On a strong or critical hit, a creature up to Huge size is swallowed. While swallowed, the creature is blinded and restrained, has total cover against attacks from outside the tarrasque, and takes 18 acid damage at the start of each of the tarrasque's turns.
- **Claw** (*slash, reach*). 18/33/48 damage.
- **Horns** (*crush, reach*). 18/33/48 damage.
- **Tail** (*crush, reach*). 18/33/48 damage. On a strong or critical hit, the target is pushed a short distance and knocked prone.

**Abilities:**

- **Legendary Resistance (Passive, 3/day).** If this creature fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Magic Resistance (Passive).** This creature has +2 boons on saves against spells and other magical effects.
- **Reflective Carapace (Passive).** Any time this creature is targeted by a magic missile spell, a line spell, or a spell that requires a ranged attack roll, roll a d6. On a 1 to 5, this creature is unaffected. On a 6, this creature is unaffected, and the effect is reflected back at the caster as though it originated from this creature.
- **Siege Monster (Passive).** This creature deals double damage to objects and structures.
- **Frightful Presence (Action).** Each creature of this creature's choice within very long range must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 16 or become frightened for a short duration. While frightened in this way, a creature is paralyzed.
- **World Destroyer (Lord Trigger).** When this creature loses a life pool, the ground trembles and cracks. All creatures within long range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 16 or take 16 damage and fall prone. Additionally, the terrain within long range becomes difficult terrain as the ground ruptures.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Chomp.** This creature makes one bite attack against a target it has grappled.
- **Move.** This creature moves up to half its speed.
- **Tail Attack.** This creature makes one tail attack.

### **Primordial Titan** (Colossal Spirit)

**Tier:** 10 (Lord)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 3x100 | 20 (natural heavy) | d12+2 | d8 | d12+1 | d10 | 18 | 13 | 17 |

**Skills:** Arcana (5), Athletics (5), Fighting (5), Fortitude (5), Insight (5), Lore (5), Perception (5)

**Immunities:** fire, cold, lightning, poison damage, charmed, frightened, poisoned

**Resistances:** physical damage

**Attacks:**

- **Titan's Fist** (*crush, reach*). 20/37/54 damage plus 8 elemental damage (choose type when attacking).
- **Elemental Storm** (*very long range, recharge (d6)*). This creature calls down elemental fury on a point it can see. All creatures within medium range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 16 or take 28 elemental damage (fire, cold, or lightning) and be knocked prone.

**Abilities:**

- **Legendary Resistance (Passive, 3/day).** If this creature fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Magic Resistance (Passive).** This creature has +2 boons on saves against spells and other magical effects.
- **Siege Monster (Passive).** This creature deals double damage to objects and structures.
- **Elemental Mastery (Passive).** At the start of each of its turns, this creature can choose one elemental damage type (fire, cold, or lightning). It becomes immune to that damage type and its attacks deal that type of damage until its next turn.
- **Innate Spellcasting (Passive).** This creature can cast the following spells once per scene each: *Control Weather*, *Earthquake*, *Fire Storm*, *Meteor Swarm*, *Plane Shift*, *Storm of Vengeance*.
- **Primordial Awakening (Lord Trigger).** When this creature loses a life pool, reality warps around it. Choose one: (1) Elemental pillars erupt in a long radius, each dealing 12 damage of a random elemental type to creatures within close range; (2) This creature summons two Huge elementals of its choice; (3) All spells and magical effects within very long range are dispelled.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Elemental Shield.** This creature surrounds itself with swirling elemental energy. Until the start of its next turn, it has +4 AV and any creature that hits it with a melee attack takes 10 elemental damage.
- **Stomp.** All creatures within close range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 16 or take 10 damage and fall prone.

### **Avatar of the Void** (Huge Aberration)

**Tier:** 10 (Lord)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 3x100 | 12 (natural light) | d10 | d12 | d12+2 | d12+1 | 16 | 17 | 18 |

**Skills:** Arcana (5), Insight (5), Lore (5), Perception (5)

**Immunities:** psychic, poison damage, charmed, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, unconscious

**Resistances:** all damage except force and radiant

**Attacks:**

- **Mind Shatter** (*range (long)*). 18/33/48 psychic damage. The target must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 16 or be stunned until the end of their next turn.
- **Reality Tear** (*line, long range*). All creatures in the line must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 16 or take 20 force damage and be teleported to a random location within medium range.
- **Void Grasp** (*reach*). 16/29/42 necrotic damage. On a hit, the target's soul is partially drawn out. They have +1 bane on all rolls until they finish a long rest.

**Abilities:**

- **Legendary Resistance (Passive, 3/day).** If this creature fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Aura of Madness (Passive).** Any creature that starts its turn within short range must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 16 or be confused until the start of its next turn.
- **Incorporeal (Passive).** This creature can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain. It takes 10 force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.
- **Innate Spellcasting (Passive).** This creature can cast the following spells once per scene each: *Dominate Monster*, *Feeblemind*, *Plane Shift*, *Power Word Kill*, *Psychic Scream*, *True Polymorph*.
- **Reality Unravels (Lord Trigger).** When this creature loses a life pool, the fabric of reality frays around it. All creatures within medium range must roll Mind + Fortitude vs. TN 16 or suffer one of the following effects (roll d4): (1) teleported to a random location within long range; (2) aged 1d10 years; (3) have one spell or ability forgotten until they finish a long rest; (4) gain a short-term madness.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Blink.** This creature teleports up to medium range to an unoccupied space it can see.
- **Psychic Lash.** One creature within short range must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 16 or take 8 psychic damage.

### **Demigod Champion** (Large Spirit)

**Tier:** 10 (Elite)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 2x100 | 20 (divine heavy) | d12+2 | d10 | d12+1 | d10 | 18 | 15 | 16 |

**Skills:** Athletics (5), Fighting (5), Fortitude (5), Influence (5), Insight (5), Perception (5)

**Immunities:** charmed, frightened, poisoned

**Resistances:** physical damage (from non-magical weapons)

**Attacks:**

- **Divine Weapon** (*versatile +2*). 20/37/54 radiant damage.
- **Smite** (*range (long)*). 18/33/48 radiant damage. On a strong or critical hit, the target is blinded until the end of their next turn.

**Abilities:**

- **Divine Aura (Passive).** Allies within short range gain +2 to all saves and are immune to fear.
- **Legendary Resistance (Passive, 2/day).** If this creature fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Magic Resistance (Passive).** This creature has +2 boons on saves against spells and other magical effects.
- **Innate Spellcasting (Passive).** This creature can cast the following spells rolling Spirit + Mysticism once per scene each: *Dispel Evil and Good*, *Greater Restoration*, *Heal*, *Holy Aura*, *Resurrection*.
- **Divine Wrath (Elite Trigger).** When this creature loses its first life pool, divine light erupts from its body. All enemy creatures within medium range must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 16 or take 20 radiant damage and be blinded for a short duration.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Inspiring Word.** One ally within medium range regains 15 HP and can immediately make an attack.
- **Divine Shield.** This creature gains +5 to all defenses until the start of its next turn.

### **The Enlightened Lich** (Medium Undead)

**Tier:** 10 (Lord)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 3x100 | 12 (natural light + arcane ward) | d8 | d10 | d12+1 | d12+2 | 15 | 16 | 18 |

**Skills:** Arcana (5), Education (5), Fighting (5), Fortitude (5), Insight (5), Lore (5), Mysticism (5), Perception (5)

**Immunities:** cold, necrotic, poison damage, bleeding, charmed, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, unconscious

**Attacks:**

- **Soul Drain** (*target: vs. Resist*). 18/33/48 necrotic damage. The target's maximum HP is reduced by the damage dealt until they finish a long rest. If this reduces their max HP to 0, they die and their soul is absorbed.
- **Annihilation Ray** (*range (very long), recharge (d4)*). 24/44/64 force damage. If this damage reduces the target to 0 HP, the target is disintegrated and cannot be restored except by true resurrection or wish.

**Abilities:**

- **Undead Nature (Passive).** This creature is immune to bleeding, being charmed, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, or falling unconscious.
- **Legendary Resistance (Passive, 3/day).** If this creature fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Magic Resistance (Passive).** This creature has +2 boons on saves against spells and other magical effects.
- **Phylactery Network (Passive).** This creature has multiple phylacteries across the world. It can only be permanently destroyed if all are found and destroyed.
- **Innate Spellcasting (Passive).** This creature can cast the following spells rolling Mind + Arcana once per scene each (and can cast any three again after each life pool is lost): *Counterspell*, *Dimension Door*, *Disintegrate*, *Dominate Monster*, *Finger of Death*, *Globe of Invulnerability*, *Meteor Swarm*, *Power Word Kill*, *Prismatic Wall*, *Time Stop*, *Wish*.
- **Transcendent Undeath (Lord Trigger).** When this creature loses a life pool, choose one: (1) All creatures within medium range must save vs. TN 16 or be paralyzed for a short duration while this creature drains their life essence, healing 30 HP; (2) This creature casts Time Stop without using a spell slot; (3) This creature summons 1d6 wights and 2d6 wraiths.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Greater Shield.** This creature can cast a powerful magical shield, gaining +6 Dodge until the start of its next turn.
- **Counterspell.** This creature can attempt to interrupt a spell being cast within medium range.
- **Teleport.** This creature teleports up to long range to an unoccupied space it can see.
