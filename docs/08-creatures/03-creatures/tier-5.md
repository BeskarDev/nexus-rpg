# Tier 5

Tier 5 creatures pose serious threats. They include powerful monstrosities, dangerous spellcasters, and formidable spirits.

---

### **Young Fire Dragon** (Large Draconic)

**Tier:** 5 (Elite)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 2x50 | 10 (natural heavy) | d10 | d8 | d10 | d8 | 12 | 10 | 11 |

**Skills:** Athletics (3), Fighting (3), Fortitude (3), Insight (3), Perception (3), Survival (3)

**Resistances:** fire damage

**Attacks:**

- **2 x Claw** (*light, slash*). 10/17/24 damage.
- **Bite** (*crush, reach*). 13/23/33 damage. On a strong or critical hit and if the target is smaller than this creature, the target is grappled and restrained.
- **Tail** (*crush, reach*). 10/17/24 damage. On a strong hit, the target is pushed close. On a critical hit, the target is pushed close and falls prone.
- **Dragon's Breath** (*recharge (d6)*). Roll Strength + Fortitude vs. Dodge. Target all creatures in short range in a cone. On a success, each target suffers 18 fire damage. On a strong or critical success, each target also suffers burning (6).

**Abilities:**

- **Flying (Passive).** This creature can fly with 2 Movement per turn. If on the ground, it can only spend 1 Movement during its turn in total.
- **Legendary Resistance (Passive, 1/day).** If this creature fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Fiery Wrath (Elite Trigger).** When this creature loses its first life pool, it immediately regains its Dragon's Breath and uses it.
- **Frightful Presence (Action).** Each creature of this creature's choice within medium range must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 11 or become frightened for a short duration. A creature can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Wing Attack.** This creature beats its wings. Each creature within melee range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 11 or take 7 damage and be knocked prone. This creature can then fly up to half its flying speed.

### **Mummy Lord** (Medium Undead)

**Tier:** 5 (Lord)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 3x50 | 6 (natural light) | d10 | d8 | d10 | d10 | 11 | 10 | 11 |

**Skills:** Arcana (3), Fighting (3), Fortitude (3), Insight (3), Lore (3), Mysticism (3), Perception (3)

**Weaknesses:** fire damage

**Resistances:** necrotic damage, physical damage (from non-magical weapons)

**Immunities:** bleeding, charmed, confused, deafened, frightened, poisoned, paralyzed, unconscious

**Attacks:**

- **Rotting Fist**. 11/19/27 necrotic damage. On a strong or critical hit, the target must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 11 or be cursed with mummy rot. The cursed target can't regain hit points, and its maximum HP decreases by 10 for each 24 hours that elapse. If the curse reduces the target's HP maximum to 0, the target dies.

**Abilities:**

- **Undead Nature (Passive).** This creature is immune to bleeding, being charmed, confused, deafened, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, or falling unconscious.
- **Dreadful Glare (Action).** This creature targets one creature within short range. If the target can see this creature, it must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 11 or be frightened until the end of this creature's next turn. If the target fails by 5 or more, it is also paralyzed for the same duration.
- **Innate Spellcasting (Passive).** This creature can cast the following spells rolling Spirit + Mysticism once per scene each: *Animate Dead*, *Blindness*, *Command*, *Curse*, *Dispel Magic*, *Harm*.
- **Rejuvenation (Passive).** A destroyed mummy lord gains a new body in 24 hours if its heart is intact, regaining all its HP. The new body appears within short range of the heart.
- **Desert's Fury (Lord Trigger).** When this creature loses a life pool, it causes a sandstorm to erupt around it. All creatures within short range must roll Agility + Athletics vs. TN 11 or take 10 damage and be blinded until the end of their next turn.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Command Undead.** One undead creature within short range can immediately make an attack or move.

### **Vampire** (Medium Undead)

**Tier:** 5 (Elite)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 2x50 | 5 (natural light) | d10 | d10 | d10 | d10 | 11 | 11 | 11 |

**Skills:** Arcana (3), Athletics (3), Fighting (3), Fortitude (3), Insight (3), Perception (3), Stealth (3)

**Weaknesses:** radiant damage, running water (20 damage when ending turn in it)

**Resistances:** necrotic damage, physical damage (from non-magical weapons)

**Immunities:** bleeding, charmed, frightened, poisoned, unconscious

**Attacks:**

- **Unarmed Strike**. 10/17/24 damage.
- **Bite** (*only on grappled, incapacitated, restrained, or willing targets*). 12/21/30 necrotic damage. The target's maximum HP is reduced by the damage dealt, and this creature regains HP equal to that amount. The reduction lasts until the target finishes a long rest.

**Abilities:**

- **Undead Nature (Passive).** This creature is immune to bleeding, being charmed, frightened, poisoned, or falling unconscious.
- **Regeneration (Passive).** This creature regains 10 HP at the start of its turn if it has at least 1 HP and isn't in sunlight or running water.
- **Spider Climb (Passive).** This creature can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.
- **Vampire Weaknesses (Passive).** This creature has the following flaws: It can't enter a residence without an invitation. It takes 10 radiant damage when starting its turn in sunlight.
- **Charm (Action).** This creature targets one humanoid within short range. The target must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 11 or be charmed. The charmed target regards this creature as a trusted friend to be heeded and protected.
- **Children of the Night (Action, 1/day).** This creature magically calls 2d4 swarms of bats or rats, or 1d6 wolves. The called creatures arrive in 1d4 rounds.
- **Blood Frenzy (Elite Trigger).** When this creature loses its first life pool, its eyes turn red and it can immediately make a Bite attack against a creature within melee range. For the rest of the scene, its attacks deal an extra 4 necrotic damage.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Misty Escape.** When reduced to 0 HP, this creature can transform into mist and move up to medium range to escape destruction.
