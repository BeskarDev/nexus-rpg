# Tier 3

Tier 3 creatures represent meaningful threats. They include more powerful undead, large predators, and experienced combatants.

---

### **Ogre** (Large Giant)

**Tier:** 3 (Basic)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 30 | 4 (light) | d10 | d6 | d4 | d4 | 10 | 8 | 9 |

**Skills:** Athletics (2), Fortitude (2), Fighting (2), Survival (2)

**Attacks:**

- **Giant Club** (*crush, fragile, heavy (d10), two-handed*). 10/16/22 damage.
- **Wide Swing** (*crush*). 7/11/15 damage against all opponents in melee range.

**Abilities:**

- **Knockback (Passive).** On a strong hit or critical hit from this creature's attacks, all targets have to roll Strength/Agility + Athletics vs. TN 9. On a failure, they are pushed close and also knocked prone.

### **Ogre Brute** (Large Giant)

**Tier:** 3 (Elite)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 2x30 | 5 (light) | d10 | d6 | d4 | d4 | 10 | 8 | 9 |

**Skills:** Athletics (2), Fortitude (2), Fighting (2), Survival (2)

**Attacks:**

- **Giant Club** (*crush, heavy (d10), two-handed*). 10/16/22 damage.
- **Wide Swing** (*crush*). 7/11/15 damage against all opponents in melee range.

**Abilities:**

- **Knockback (Passive).** On a strong hit or critical hit from this creature's attacks, all targets have to roll Strength/Agility + Athletics vs. TN 9. On a failure, they are pushed close and also knocked prone.
- **Ear-shattering Roar (Elite Trigger).** When this creature loses its first life pool, it immediately lets loose an ear-shattering roar. Every enemy within short distance has to roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 9. If they fail, they take 6 sonic damage (ignoring AV) and are frightened of this creature. They can re-roll against their fear at the start of their turns.

### **Large Fire Elemental** (Large Spirit)

**Tier:** 3 (Basic)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 30 | 4 (natural light) | d10 | d10 | d8 | d6 | 9 | 10 | 9 |

**Skills:** Fighting (2), Fortitude (2), Perception (2)

**Weaknesses:** frost damage, damage from water

**Resistances:** physical damage

**Immunities:** fire damage, burning, bleeding, poisoned, grappled, restrained, stunned, unconscious

**Attacks:**

- **Fast Strikes.** This creature can attack or cast spells twice per turn, unless it uses Incineration.
- **Burning Lash**. 10/16/22 fire damage. On a strong or critical hit, inflicts burning (6) for a short duration.
- **Flame Bolt** (*range (medium)*). 9/14/19 fire damage. On a strong or critical hit, inflicts burning (4) for a short duration.
- **Incineration** (*blast (short), recharge (d6)*). Spirit + Fortitude vs. Dodge. 9/14/19 fire damage against each target. On a strong or critical hit, inflicts burning (4) for a short duration.

**Abilities:**

- **Ethereal Form (Passive).** This creature is immune to bleeding, poison damage, being poisoned, being grappled, restrained, stunned, or falling unconscious.
- **Flame Body (Passive).** This creature is immune to fire damage and burning. This creature illuminates the area in short distance around it in bright light. If this creature is hit by an attack at melee range, the attacker suffers 6 fire damage (ignoring AV). If this creature comes into contact with a large amount of water, it takes 5 damage (ignoring AV) each turn doing so.

**Armor:** Light

|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|

**Tier:** 3 (Basic)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 30 | 3 (natural light) | d6 | d10 | d8 | d6 | 8 | 10 | 9 |

**Skills:** Fighting (2), Perception (2), Influence (2)

**Attacks:**

- **Claws** (*agile*). 7/11/15 damage.
- **Club** (*crush*). 7/11/15 damage.

**Abilities:**

- **Flying (Passive).** This creature can fly with 2 Movement per turn. If on the ground, it can only spend 1 Movement during its turn in total.
- **Luring Song (Action).** This creature sings a magical melody. Every humanoid within medium range that can hear the song must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 9 or be charmed until the song ends. Charmed targets must use their movement to move toward this creature by the most direct route and can't willingly move away. A target can repeat the save when it takes damage.
