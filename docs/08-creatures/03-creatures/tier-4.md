# Tier 4

Tier 4 creatures are formidable opponents requiring tactical thinking. They include dangerous beasts, regenerating giants, and elite fighters.

---

### **Troll** (Large Giant)

**Tier:** 4 (Basic)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 40 | 5 (natural light) | d10 | d8 | d8 | d6 | 11 | 9 | 10 |

**Skills:** Athletics (3), Fortitude (3), Fighting (2), Perception (3), Survival (3)

**Attacks:**

- **2 x Claw** (*light*). 9/14/19 damage. On a hit, attempts to grapple the target.
- **Bite** (*only if grappling*). 11/18/25 damage. On a hit, the target is poisoned for a short duration.
- **Poisonous Spit** (*range (medium)*). 8/12/16 poison damage. On a hit, the target is briefly poisoned.

**Abilities:**

- **Keen Scent (Passive).** This creature gains +1 boon on Perception rolls based on smell.
- **Regeneration (Passive).** This creature regains 10 HP at the start of each of its turns, unless it has taken fire or acid damage since its last turn. This creature even regenerates while at 0 HP and only dies from its Wounds, if it can't regenerate at the start of its turn.

### **Young Fire Dragon** (Large Draconic)

**Tier:** 4 (Elite)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 2x40 | 8 (natural heavy) | d10 | d8 | d8 | d6 | 11 | 9 | 10 |

**Skills:** Athletics (2), Fighting (3), Fortitude (3), Perception (2), Survival (2)

**Resistances:** fire damage

**Attacks:**

- **2 x Claw** (*light, slash*). 9/14/19 damage.
- **Bite** (*crush, reach*). 11/18/25 damage. On a strong or critical hit and if the target is smaller than this creature, the target is grappled and restrained. This creature cannot use its Bite attack against other targets while grappling a creature.

**Abilities:**

- **Flying (Passive).** This creature can fly with 2 Movement per turn. If on the ground, it can only spend 1 Movement during its turn in total.
- **Fiery Wrath (Elite Trigger).** When this creature loses its first life pool, it immediately regains its Dragon's Breath and uses it.

### **Nakesh Cult Leader** (Medium Humanoid)

**Tier:** 4 (Lord)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 3x40 | 4 (natural light) | d8 | d8 | d6 | d10 | 10 | 10 | 11 |

**Skills:** Arcana (3), Influence (3), Fighting (2), Lore (3), Perception (3)

**Resistances:** poison damage

**Immunities:** poisoned

**Attacks:**

- **Serpent Scepter** (*crush, reach, versatile +1*). Roll attacks with Mind + Arcana. 9/14/19 force damage. On a strong hit, the target is pushed close. On a critical hit, the target is pushed a short distance away instead.
- **Poison Mist** (*target: vs. Dodge, range: short*). Roll Mind + Arcana. 9/14/19 poison damage. On a strong hit, the target is briefly poisoned. On a critical hit, the target is poisoned for a short duration instead. They can roll Strength + Fortitude vs. TN 10 at the end of their turns to end the effect early.

**Abilities:**

- **Blindsight (Close) (Passive).** This creature can perceive its surroundings at close range without relying on sight.
- **Innate Spellcasting (Passive).** This creature can cast the following spells rolling Mind + Arcana once per scene each: *Attack Thoughts*, *Control Beast* (only snakes), *Subtle Suggestion*, *Forced Suggestion*, *Cloud of Sickness*, *Nullify Spell*.
- **Shapeshifting (Action).** Using an Action, this creature can assume the form of any other humanoid creature of the same size. If this creature falls unconscious or is killed, they revert to their own form.
- **Serpentine Retreat (Lord Trigger).** When this creature loses a life pool, it bursts into a cluster of snakes, dealing 8 poison damage to all creatures in melee range of it and it can move up to a long distance. Other creatures can't use Quick Actions or other abilities in response to this ability. After moving, this creature can cast its spells again as if it were a new scene.

### **General** (Medium Humanoid)

**Tier:** 4 (Elite)
**Armor:** Heavy

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 2x40 | 8 (heavy + helmet + shield) | d10 | d8 | d8 | d8 | 11 | 9 | 10 |

**Skills:** Athletics (3), Education (3), Fighting (3), Fortitude (3), Influence (3), Insight (3), Perception (3)

**Attacks:**

- **Fast Strikes.** This creature can attack twice per turn.
- **Longsword** (*pierce, versatile +1*). 10/16/22 damage.
- **Heavy Shield** (*AV +2, parry +1, rigid 1*). 8/12/16 damage.
- **Crossbow** (*ammo, heavy (d8), range (medium), reload, two-handed*). 11/18/25 damage.

**Abilities:**

- **Tactical Genius (Action).** This creature can command up to five allies within medium range to immediately move a short distance or take a single Quick Action. Each ally can only be commanded once per turn.
- **Inspiring Presence (Passive).** Allies within short range of this creature gain +1 boon on Morale checks and saves against fear and +2 damage on attacks.
- **Battle Cry (Elite Trigger).** When this creature loses its first life pool, all allies within medium range can immediately make a single attack or move a short distance.

**Quick Actions:**

- **Second Wind.** Once per combat, this creature can use its Quick Action on its turn to regain 15 HP.
- **Parry.** This creature can use its Quick Action to gain +2 Parry until the start of its next turn.

### **Mummy** (Medium Undead)

**Tier:** 4 (Basic)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 40 | 5 (natural light) | d10 | d6 | d8 | d6 | 10 | 9 | 10 |

**Skills:** Fighting (3), Fortitude (3), Insight (3), Perception (3)

**Weaknesses:** fire damage

**Resistances:** necrotic damage, physical damage (from non-magical weapons)

**Immunities:** bleeding, charmed, confused, deafened, frightened, poisoned, unconscious

**Attacks:**

- **Rotting Fist**. 10/16/22 necrotic damage. On a strong or critical hit, the target must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 10 or be cursed with mummy rot. The cursed target can't regain hit points, and its maximum HP decreases by 10 for each 24 hours that elapse. If the curse reduces the target's HP maximum to 0, the target dies.

**Abilities:**

- **Undead Nature (Passive).** This creature is immune to bleeding, being charmed, confused, deafened, frightened, poisoned, or falling unconscious.
- **Dreadful Glare (Action).** This creature targets one creature within short range. If the target can see this creature, it must roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 10 or be frightened until the end of this creature's next turn. If the target fails by 5 or more, it is also paralyzed for the same duration.

### **Medusa** (Medium Monstrosity)

**Tier:** 4 (Basic)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 40 | 5 (natural light) | d8 | d10 | d8 | d8 | 10 | 10 | 10 |

**Skills:** Fighting (3), Insight (3), Perception (3), Stealth (3)

**Attacks:**

- **Snake Hair** (*pierce*). 9/14/19 poison damage. On a strong or critical hit, the target is also poisoned for a short duration.
- **Shortbow** (*ammo, range (medium), two-handed*). 9/14/19 damage.

**Abilities:**

- **Petrifying Gaze (Action).** When a creature that can see this creature's eyes starts its turn within short range of this creature, this creature can force it to roll Spirit + Fortitude vs. TN 10. On a failure, the creature is restrained as it begins to turn to stone. The restrained creature must repeat the save at the end of its next turn, becoming petrified on a failure or ending the effect on a success. Unless surprised, a creature can avert its eyes at the start of its turn to avoid making this save, but attacks against this creature have +1 bane until its next turn.
