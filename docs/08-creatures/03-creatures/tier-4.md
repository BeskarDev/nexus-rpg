# Tier 4

Tier 4 creatures are formidable opponents requiring tactical thinking. They include dangerous beasts, regenerating giants, and elite fighters.

---

### **Troll** (Large Giant)

**Tier:** 4 (Basic)
**Armor:** Light

| HP | AV | STR | AGI | SPI | MND | Parry | Dodge | Resist |
|----|----|----|----|----|-----|-------|-------|--------|
| 40 | 5 (natural light) | d10 | d8 | d8 | d6 | 11 | 9 | 10 |

**Skills:** Athletics (3), Fortitude (3), Fighting (2), Perception (3), Survival (3)

**Attacks:**

- **2 x Claw** (*light*). 9/14/19 damage. On a hit, attempts to grapple the target.
- **Bite** (*only if grappling*). 11/18/25 damage. On a hit, the target is poisoned for a short duration.
- **Poisonous Spit** (*range (medium)*). 8/12/16 poison damage. On a hit, the target is briefly poisoned.

**Abilities:**

- **Keen Scent (Passive).** This creature gains +1 boon on Perception rolls based on smell.
- **Regeneration (Passive).** This creature regains 10 HP at the start of each of its turns, unless it has taken fire or acid damage since its last turn. This creature even regenerates while at 0 HP and only dies from its Wounds, if it can't regenerate at the start of its turn.

**Tier:** 4 (Lord)

**Attacks:**

- **Innate Spellcasting (Passive).** This creature can cast the following spells rolling Mind + Arcana or Spirit + Mysticism once per scene each: *Attack Thoughts*, *Subtle Suggestion*, *Forced Suggestion*, *Cloud of Sickness*, *Animate Dead*, *Curse*.
