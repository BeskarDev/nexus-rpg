## Healing Recipes

### Healing Salve (Weak) - Q1
**Ingredients:** Common moss (1) + Animal fat (5)  
**Cost:** 7 coins  
**Craft:** TN 6, 1 success, 10 coins/week  
**Effect:** Apply to treated Wound. Target gains +1 boon on next healing roll  

### Healing Salve (Simple) - Q2
**Ingredients:** Silverleaf (12) + Beeswax (10) + Water (3)  
**Cost:** 15 coins  
**Craft:** TN 6, 2 successes, 20 coins/week  
**Effect:** Apply to Wound. +2 boons on next healing roll  

### Healing Salve (Potent) - Q3
**Ingredients:** Vitality flower (80) + Refined pitch (40) + Crushed gems (50)  
**Cost:** 62 coins  
**Craft:** TN 8, 4 successes, 50 coins/week  
**Effect:** Apply to Wound. Auto-heals during next night's rest  

### Potion of Healing (Weak) - Q3
**Ingredients:** Vitality flower (80) + Elemental essence (80) + Honey (10)  
**Cost:** 62 coins  
**Effect:** Drink to regain 8 HP  

### Potion of Healing (Decent) - Q4
**Ingredients:** Troll blood (260) + Crystal powder (220) + Pitch (40) + Celestial salt (250)  
**Cost:** 187 coins  
**Craft:** TN 10, 6 successes, 200 coins/week  
**Effect:** Drink to regain 16 HP

**Cost:** 625 coins  
**Craft:** TN 12, 8 successes, 1,000 coins/week  
**Effect:** Drink to regain 24 HP

## Custom Recipes

### Creating New Recipes

1. **Choose effect and Quality**
2. **Select ingredient tags:**
   - 1 Essence (matches effect)
   - 1 Catalyst (amplifies)
   - Optional: Binder (duration), more for special effects
3. **Pick specific ingredients at appropriate potency**
4. **Calculate costs** per standard crafting
5. **Get GM approval**

### Example Custom Creation

**Potion of Invisibility (Q5)**
- Effect: Become invisible (short). Ends if attack/cast/damage
- Ingredients:
  - Ghostcap fungus (Essence: invisibility, Q3, 90)
  - Fairy dust (Essence: enchantment, Q5, 850)
  - Moonwell water (Catalyst: lunar, Q5, 900)
  - Diamond dust (Reagent, Q5, 1,000)
- Quality: Q5 (limited by lowest high-tier ingredient)
- Cost: 625 coins material, TN 12, 8 successes, 1,000 coins/week
