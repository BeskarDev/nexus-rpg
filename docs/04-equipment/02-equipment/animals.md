# Animals

## Animals

| Quality | Name | Load | Cost | Description |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Animal Companion |  | 75 | A wolf, large cat, or similar predator trained from youth to serve as a loyal companion. Bonds with one master and follows complex commands. Requires the Nature talent “Animal Companion” to control such a creature. |
| 1 | Draft Horse |  | 75 | A largely untrained horse (can also be a donkey or mule), fit to hold gear or pull a vehicle but not to be ridden. Can hold up to 20 load. See under Creatures to see its statistics. |
| 1 | Guard Dog |  | 75 | A dog or other similar-sized animal that underwent basic training to fight and protect its owner. Can be trained as a tame animal companion, provided you have the Survival talent with the same name. |
| 2 | Camel |  | 250 | Can be ridden by up to two medium creatures. For each creature not riding, the camel can hold up to 15 load instead (so max. 30 load). |
| 2 | Riding Horse |  | 250 | A well-trained horse suitable for riding and light combat. Can carry a rider plus up to 10 load. Responds to voice and leg commands. |
| 2 | Trained Hawk/Eagle |  | 250 | A bird of prey trained for hunting and scouting. Can deliver messages over short distances and hunt small game. |
| 3 | War Horse |  | 750 | A large, aggressive horse trained for battle. Can carry a rider plus up to 15 load. Trained to fight alongside its rider and strike enemies with hooves. |
