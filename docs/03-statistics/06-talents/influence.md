# Influence

Name | Description
--- | ---
**Born Haggler**  | <strong>(Rank 1)</strong> When selling an item, you receive 60% of it’s cost instead of 50%.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 2) </strong>When searching for rare items during downtime, you can re-roll the test once per activity.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 3)</strong> When selling an item, you receive 75% of it’s cost instead of 50%.
**Fast Talking**  | (<strong>Rank 1) </strong>When you fail an Influence roll in any social situation, you can choose to re-roll the test once per scene.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 2)</strong> You can also use your Fast Talking ability to assist an ally in conversation. When doing so, they can add your Influence instead of their own to a roll.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 3)</strong> Immediately before combat begins and if you and your enemies see, hear, and understand each other, you can attempt to verbally confuse them.<br/>Roll Mind + Influence vs. Resist against all enemies hearing and understanding you. On a success, their Initiative is reduced by one category and they are briefly dazed.
**Inspire Ally**  | <strong>(Rank 1) </strong>You can use your Quick Action to inspire an ally that can see and hear you within medium range. When you do so, they gain +1 boon on one test they are about to roll.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 2) </strong>You gain the following abilities:<br/>- Once per scene, you can use Inspire Ally without using your Quick Action. You can still use the ability only once per roll.<br/>- When the inspired test is a successful attack roll, the ally also deals +2 damage (situational bonus) with it.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 3)</strong> You gain the following abilities:<br/>- You can also use Inspire Ally on yourself.<br/>- The damage bonus from Inspire Ally increases to +4.<br/>- Whenever you use Inspire Ally, the target also regains 4 HP.
**Insult to Injury**  | <strong>(Rank 1) </strong>After you hit an enemy with an attack, you can use a Quick Action to use Distract with +1 boon against them.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 2)</strong> While an enemy is distracted by you, you deal +2 damage (situational bonus) against them. When anyone but you deals damage to them, the distract condition still ends early.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 3)</strong> You gain the following abilities:<br/>- The damage bonus from Insult to Injury increases to +4.<br/>- When an enemy that is distracted by you would lose the condition because they took damage from another creature than you, you can use a Quick Action to immediately use Distract against them again to attempt to continue the distraction.
**Leading Presence**  | (<strong>Rank 1)</strong> While you can see and hear each other, allies in short distance of you gain the following effects:<br/>- You gain +1 boon on your Initiative roll.<br/>- You gain +1 boon on Spirit rolls against being frightened or losing morale.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 2)</strong> While you can see and hear each other, allies in short distance of you gain the following effects:<br/>- You gain +1 Resist (ability bonus).<br/>- You deal +2 damage with any attack.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 3)</strong> You also gain the effects of your Leading Presence ability and it extends to all allies in long distance of you.
**Performer**  | <strong>(Rank 1) </strong>Once per day, you can perform music, poetry, a story, or a dance for a short duration. Describe what your performance is about and how it speaks to those watching. Roll Spirit + Influence and choose up to four other creatures who witnessed the performance.<br/><strong>Weak.</strong> Each affected creature gains +1 boon on any one roll they make before the end of that day.<br/><strong>Strong.</strong> Each affected creature also gains 5 temporary HP for the rest of the day.<br/><strong>Critical.</strong> Once per adventure, one affected creature of your choice also gains +1 Resolve.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 2)</strong> You gain the following abilities:<br/>- When you meet an NPC in a social situation, you can open with a song, a tale, or a verse composed in their honor before any serious talk begins. Roll Spirit + Influence vs. their Resist. On a success, their starting Interest in this Social Intrigue increases by 1. You can attempt this once per NPC per scene.<br/>- While traveling, you keep the group's spirits up with marching songs and tales. Once per day, when an ally would resolve one or more consequences from a failure or blunder on their travel roll, you can negate one consequence of their choice.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 3)</strong> You gain the following abilities:<br/>- Your daily performance also affects you, in addition to the chosen creatures.<br/>- When your daily performance grants +1 Resolve on a critical success, all affected creatures gain it instead of only one.<br/>- When you take the Carouse downtime activity, you perform for the crowds. You gain +1 boon on the roll and don't have to pay the expenses of the activity. Up to four allies taking the same activity in the same settlement also gain +1 boon on their rolls.
**Presence of Conquest**  | <em>Requires Influence at rank 4 and the Leading Presence talent at Rank 3.</em><br/><br/><strong>(Rank 4)</strong> Once per scene, after you hit an enemy with an attack or reduce an enemy to 0 HP, you can use your Quick Action to project overwhelming dominance. Roll Spirit + Influence once against the Resist of each enemy within close range that can see and hear you. On a success against a target, they are briefly dazed. On a strong or critical success against a target, they are briefly frightened instead.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 5)</strong> Once per day, you can use your Action to unleash the full weight of your presence in a roar or display of conquest. Roll Spirit + Influence once against the Resist of each enemy within short range that can see and hear you. On a weak success against a target, they are briefly dazed. On a strong success, they are briefly frightened instead. On a critical success, they are briefly stunned instead.
**Rallying Cry**  | <strong>(Rank 1)</strong> Once per scene, you can use your Quick Action to call out to one ally within short range who can see and hear you. They can immediately end the frightened or dazed condition on themselves. If they suffer from neither, they instead gain +1 boon on their next roll.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 2)</strong> You gain the following abilities:<br/>- Your Rallying Cry can also end the confused condition.<br/>- The boon from your Rallying Cry applies whether or not the ally cleared a condition.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 3)</strong> When you use your Rallying Cry, it affects all allies within short range who can see and hear you instead of only one.
**Silver Tongue**  | <strong>(Rank 1)</strong> Once per scene, you can keep a faltering conversation alive, holding the attention of someone who would otherwise grow impatient or walk away. If you are resolving a Social Intrigue, you can instead keep the Patience die from advancing for one exchange.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 2)</strong> When you successfully appeal to one of the NPC's Motivations (a desire, fear, bond, or value), you gain +1 boon on your next Influence roll against them.<br/><br/><strong>(Rank 3)</strong> Once per scene, when you or an ally commits a social misstep that would turn the person you are dealing with against you, you can smooth it over before it lands, and their attitude toward you doesn't sour. If you are resolving a Social Intrigue, the NPC's Interest doesn't drop instead, and a Breakdown that drop would have caused doesn't occur.
