### Dream Realm

You burn dream-incense and lie down among the sleepers, and step sideways into a realm of your own shaping, a twilight country of still water and soft stars where minds may meet. The rite consumes 5000 coins worth of dream-incense and offerings whether or not it succeeds.
On a success, you draw yourself and up to six willing creatures who sleep near you, or who you are connected to across any distance within the same sphere of existence, into the shared dream for as long as you remain asleep within it. Within the dream, apply the following:
- All who share the dream can see and speak with one another freely, however far apart their sleeping bodies lie, and remember all that passed on waking.
- You may shape the dream's country as you wish, calling up a remembered place for the sleepers to walk, so that a memory can be revisited and examined together.

The dream shortens a rest, it never grants a second one, and it never counts as a full rest.
The dream cannot harm those within it, and it ends for any sleeper the moment their body wakes. Sleeping bodies remain in the waking world, defenseless and unaware, for the whole of the rite.

### Shadowform

You breathe out and unmake your body into a thin ribbon of living shadow, cool and silent, that pours along walls and under doors.
On a success, you take shadowform for a short duration while you concentrate, and gain the following:
- You are immune to physical damage and cannot be grappled, restrained, or knocked prone.
- You can pass through any solid object as though it were open air, and you move up to a close distance in any direction each turn, clinging to surfaces and slipping through the narrowest seam.
- You gain +1 boon on rolls to cast *twilight* spells.
- When you make a physical attack or cast a spell that deals damage, you must gather substance for the act. Until the start of your next turn you have resistance to physical damage instead of immunity to it.
- You cannot carry or manipulate any object beyond a weapon you already hold.
- You carry the gloom with you. The area within melee range of you is dimmed and counts as lightly obscured, and you yourself always stand in shadow, whatever the light around you.
- Mundane light, however bright, cannot force you from this form. Only magical bright light that expels darkness forces you back into flesh and ends this spell.
You can end the spell at will, returning to your body in an open space you occupy.

### Phantom World

You draw the dreaming out over the waking world, and within a stretch of ground the land itself becomes a lie of your telling: false walls, false faces, false paths, all obedient to your will.
On a success, the phantom world fills the area in medium range of the target location for a medium duration while you concentrate. You choose what every creature inside perceives, reshaping the seeming of terrain, structures, and faces as you direct, and you may change it on each of your turns.
Target each creature within the area, using your roll's result vs. their Resist. On a success, that creature is affected by the phantom world. On a failure, they perceive the truth as a faint shimmer over the lie. When a creature enters the area for the first time later, roll Spirit + Mysticism vs. their Resist to determine this instead. Creatures of a tier higher than your Mysticism and creatures without a mind are never affected.
An affected creature perceives the phantom world as real and acts on it, though the substance beneath is unchanged: a phantom wall stops no one who does not believe in it, and no illusion within deals true harm.
At the start of each of your turns, choose any number of affected creatures. The false world gnaws at their minds and they take 6 psychic damage (ignore AV).
Once per turn when you reshape the phantom world, choose one affected creature. They are briefly confused as the lie closes in around them.
When a confused creature within the phantom world rolls their Confusion Check, you choose the result instead of rolling.
A creature that sees through the illusion, per its illusory property, is freed from the phantom world and all of its effects, and perceives the truth as a faint shimmer over the lie.
