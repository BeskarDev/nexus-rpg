Mystic magic represents reverent spiritual channeling, drawing power from divine forces, natural spirits, and cosmic principles. Mystic casters (Mystics) act as conduits for powers greater than themselves, respecting the natural order while wielding its might.

## Mystic Traditions

Mystic magic is organized into eight traditions, each representing a different spiritual philosophy and source of power:

### [Light](./light)
**Identity**: Reverent revelation and purification  
**Focus**: Support, Offense, Healing  
**Damage Types**: Radiant, Fire, Blast

The tradition of truth, illumination, and divine judgment. Light spells reveal lies and invisibility, purify corruption, harm undead, and provide guidance through radiance.

### [Twilight](./twilight)
**Identity**: Respectful balance and mystery  
**Focus**: Utility, Control, Support  
**Damage Types**: Psychic, Frost

Masters of dreams, secrets, and fate. Twilight spells induce sleep, grant prophetic visions, manipulate shadows, enable transformation, and read the threads of destiny.

### [Life](./life)
**Identity**: Communal growth and restoration  
**Focus**: Healing, Support, Utility  
**Damage Types**: Radiant, Psychic

The tradition of vitality, blessings, and hope. Life spells heal wounds, cure diseases, bless allies, promote growth, and strengthen communal bonds.

### [Death](./death)
**Identity**: Natural ending and ancestral respect  
**Focus**: Control, Offense, Utility  
**Damage Types**: Necrotic, Poison, Acid

The power of natural endings and ancestral wisdom. Death spells cause disease, place curses, inspire fear, accelerate decay, and commune with ancestors and the dead.

### [Nature](./nature)
**Identity**: Harmonious adaptation with natural world  
**Focus**: Utility, Healing, Support  
**Damage Types**: Physical, Poison, Acid

The tradition of wilderness and primal forces. Nature spells communicate with animals, control plants, influence weather, create environmental hazards, and provide herbal remedies.

### [Tempest](./tempest)
**Identity**: Respectful wielding of destructive natural forces  
**Focus**: Offense, Control, Utility  
**Damage Types**: Lightning, Frost, Blast

The power of storms, earthquakes, and natural disasters. Tempest spells summon hurricanes, call lightning, create earthquakes, flood areas, and command the wind.

### [Peace](./peace)
**Identity**: Communal safety and lawful order  
**Focus**: Defense, Support, Healing  
**Damage Types**: Physical, Blast, Psychic

The tradition of sanctuary, calm, and protection. Peace spells create safe havens, calm emotions, establish protective barriers, ensure safe travel, and bind oaths.

### [War](./war)
**Identity**: Honorable combat prowess and righteous victory  
**Focus**: Offense, Control, Support  
**Damage Types**: Physical, Fire, Blast

The power of righteous battle and martial glory. War spells bless weapons, grant combat fury, provide tactical advantages, inspire allies through battle cries, and channel honorable bloodshed.

## Casting Mystic Spells

- **Casting Roll**: Spirit + Mysticism vs. Target Number or vs. enemy Defense
- **Focus Cost**: Each spell has a Focus cost that must be paid when casting
- **Penance**: If you fail to cast a mystic spell, you may lose access to spells until you perform penance (roll on Penance table)
- **Attributes**: Mystic magic is reverent and communal - it represents spiritual connection and respect for forces greater than oneself
