# ⚗️ Field Alchemy

Field alchemy is quick, improvised crafting during adventures. Working by firelight or crouched over a bronze mortar, a practiced hand turns raw materials into a single finished alchemical item.

Field alchemy and workshop alchemy are separate crafts. The Alchemist crafting profession covers workshop production during downtime (see the Craft an Item activity). Creating alchemical items in the field is the domain of talents.

## Requirements

To perform field alchemy, you need all of the following:

- **A granting talent.** Field alchemy is only available through talents such as *Herbalist*, *Poison Maker*, or *Bomb Maker*. The talent states which skill you roll, which categories of alchemical items you can create, and up to which Quality.
- **Alchemist's Supplies.** Your portable workstation. It isn't consumed by crafting, but when you fail a crafting roll, it loses 1 use.
- **Materials.** Each attempt spends 1 use of a materials supply item. The materials must be of the alchemical item's Quality or higher. Alchemical items of Quality 2 or lower can be made from materials of any Quality.

## Procedure

Field alchemy takes one exploration turn of uninterrupted work and creates a single alchemical item. Any item you create this way is unstable and loses its potency at the end of the day.

Choose an item from the catalog below that your talent allows, spend 1 use of your materials, and roll the talent's skill vs. the TN for the item's Quality.

| Item Quality | TN |
| --- | --- |
| 1 - 2 | 6 |
| 3 | 8 |
| 4 | 10 |
| 5 | 12 |

On a success, you create the item. On a failure, the materials are wasted and your Alchemist's Supplies lose 1 use. On a blunder, the same happens and you also roll on the Mishap Table.

### Mishap Table

| d6 | Mishap |
| --- | --- |
| 1 - 2 | **Toxic fumes.** You suffer 1 Fatigue. |
| 3 - 4 | **Spilled reagents.** Your materials lose 1 additional use. |
| 5 - 6 | **Caustic mess.** Your Alchemist's Supplies lose 1 additional use. |

## Item Catalog

Every alchemical item belongs to a category. Your granting talent states which categories you can create items from and up to which Quality.

### Remedies

Fresh preparations from herbs and minerals that heal and restore.

| Quality | Item | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 2 | Herbal Remedy (weak) | A creature can consume this as an Action to regain 8 HP. |
| 3 | Herbal Remedy (potent) | A creature can consume this as an Action to regain 12 HP. |
| 3 | Herbal Antidote | A creature can consume this as an Action to end one poison or natural disease affecting them. |
| 3 | Stimulant | A creature can consume this as an Action to lose 1 Fatigue. Each creature can only benefit from a stimulant once per day. |
| 4 | Fortifying Tonic | A creature can consume this as an Action to gain 10 temporary HP for a short duration. |
| 4 | Focus Tincture | A creature can consume this as an Action to regain 4 Focus. |

### Poisons

Venoms and toxic brews. Each of these matches the equipment item of the same name and follows its full rules (see the Alchemy equipment list).

| Quality | Item | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 2 | Weapon Poison (weak) | Weapon coating. On a hit, deal 2 poison damage (ignoring AV). |
| 2 | Paralyzing Poison (weak) | Weapon coating. On a hit, the target is poisoned for a short duration. |
| 3 | Weapon Poison (decent) | Weapon coating. On a hit, deal 4 poison damage (ignoring AV). |
| 3 | Paralyzing Poison (decent) | Weapon coating. On a hit, the target is poisoned and staggered for a short duration. |
| 4 | Weapon Poison (strong) | Weapon coating. On a hit, deal 6 poison damage (ignoring AV). |
| 5 | Paralyzing Poison (strong) | Weapon coating. On a hit, the target is poisoned and stunned for a short duration. |

### Bombs

Volatile vessels, explosive mixtures, and incendiary pastes. Each of these matches the equipment item of the same name and follows its full rules (see the Alchemy equipment list).

| Quality | Item | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 2 | Acid Vial | Thrown weapon. Deals +3 acid damage as weapon damage. |
| 2 | Flammable Paste | Weapon coating that can be ignited by any flame. While burning, deal 2 fire damage (ignoring AV) on a hit. |
| 2 | Thunderstone | Thrown weapon. Explodes with a thunderous crack. Creatures in melee range of the impact must pass a Strength + Fortitude test or be briefly deafened and dazed. |
| 3 | Alchemist's Fire | Thrown weapon. Deals +2 fire damage as weapon damage. On a hit, the target is also burning (2) for a short duration. |
| 3 | Fire Bomb | Thrown weapon. Deals +3 fire damage as weapon damage to all creatures in melee range of the target. |
| 3 | Smoke Bomb | Thrown weapon. Erupts in a cloud of smoke in close range of the impact for a short duration. The smoke counts as an area with dim light. |
| 3 | Tanglefoot Bag | Thrown weapon. On a hit, the target is restrained by sticky tar until they break free. The tar crumbles after a short duration. |
| 4 | Naphtha Bomb | Thrown weapon. Deals +3 fire damage as weapon damage to all creatures in melee range of the target. Damaged creatures are also burning (2) for a short duration. |
| 5 | Inferno Bomb | Thrown weapon. Deals +6 fire damage as weapon damage to all creatures in close range of the target. Damaged creatures are also burning (2) for a short duration. |

> Example: Tadese (Mind d8, Nature 2, Poison Maker Rank 1) wants to coat his arrows before a hunt. As part of a rest, he spends 1 use of his primitive materials and rolls Mind + Nature vs. TN 6 for a Weapon Poison (weak, Quality 2). He succeeds and gains the poison.
